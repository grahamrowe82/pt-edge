## Synthesis: What Practitioners Are Actually Asking AI About

### Persona clusters inferred from access patterns:

1. **Quantitative finance / algorithmic trading** -- vectorbt (14 fetches), torchquant, scikit-survival comparisons. People evaluating open-source backtesting and financial ML frameworks.

2. **ML operations / reliability engineers** -- Anomaly-Transformer, MTAD-GAT, concept drift datasets, anomaly-detection-systems category. Looking for production anomaly detection.

3. **Medical / clinical ML researchers** -- chest X-ray pathology, pathology whole-slide data, brain tumor detection. Evaluating open-source medical imaging tools.

4. **Agent builders** -- agent-governance-toolkit, hermes-plugins, marketing-agent-blueprints, agentcrumbs, agent-memory-systems. People building AI agents looking for orchestration and memory.

5. **Search / retrieval practitioners** -- clip-as-service, bm25s, semantic-search-models, code-repository-intelligence. Evaluating embedding and search infrastructure.

6. **OCR / document processing engineers** -- DocTR, deep-text-recognition-benchmark, mmocr. Document digitization pipeline evaluation.

### Strategic implications:

1. **The homepage gets 57 fetches from ChatGPT alone** -- people are asking ChatGPT "what is PT-Edge?" or similar. The homepage must answer that question crisply for AI consumption.

2. **MCP pages get zero AI-agent fetches.** This is the biggest gap. PT-Edge's core MCP focus is invisible to AI-mediated practitioners. Either the content isn't ranking for relevant queries, or practitioners aren't yet asking AI about MCP servers.

3. **ml-frameworks dominates by volume** (71%) largely because it has the most indexed pages. But the specific projects fetched reveal high-value practitioner problems: financial ML, anomaly detection, medical imaging, OCR.

4. **Category pages are underserved relative to their intent value.** Only ~5% of fetches, but these are "help me decide" queries. Enriching category pages with stronger recommendations would directly serve practitioners.

5. **OpenAI owns the channel** at 99% of traffic. Optimizing for how ChatGPT/OAI-SearchBot discovers and surfaces PT-Edge content is the highest-leverage move.

6. **Global, round-the-clock usage** with US-evening and European-morning peaks. No single timezone dominates.

7. **Comparison pages get real traffic** (visible in top 50). "X vs Y" queries are high-intent evaluation signals.
