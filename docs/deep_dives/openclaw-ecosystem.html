OpenClaw went from zero to one of the fastest-growing open-source projects in AI in a matter of weeks. What started as a personal AI assistant has spawned an entire ecosystem: clients for every platform, skill libraries, agent templates, voice assistants, deployment tools, and communities building in dozens of languages.

PT-Edge tracks 1,299 OpenClaw-related repositories across 13 categories. Here's a guide to what people are building.
