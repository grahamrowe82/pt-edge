Where to build

The quality gap of {{ cats.get('transformers:lora-qlora-fine-tuning', {}).quality_gap_score }}/100 is the highest of any Prime-tier category in our entire dataset. That means:

  • The general-purpose space is consolidating. LlamaFactory, unsloth, and PEFT are pulling away. Building another general LoRA framework is not the opportunity.
  • Multi-LoRA serving is underbuilt. LoRAX is the only serious option and it was last pushed {{ repos.get('predibase/lorax', {}).last_pushed_at | timeago }}. The gap between "fine-tune a model" and "serve it in production" is where practitioners struggle most.
  • Domain-specific fine-tuning tooling barely exists. Fine-tuning for code, for medical text, for legal documents, for structured output — each needs specialised data pipelines, evaluation metrics, and configuration patterns. The content extraction layer feeds directly into this.
  • Evaluation is the missing piece. How do you know your fine-tuned model is actually better? The evolutionary AI approach of iterative improvement through automated evaluation is one answer. The tooling for fine-tuning evaluation is thin.
